Why learn to code?

Coding is the new literacy. It is such a helpful skill in the new world. You can create anything you want, your imagination is the only limit. You can make a website, applications, games, or anything else you want. Coding can also boost your logic skills, along with looking very good on your resume, and boost skills needed for jobs.
